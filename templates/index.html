Schemes SG

One stop directory to quickly search for assistance schemes in Singapore, from government agencies, to VWOs, charities, self-help groups and more.

Get started
[Beta] Schemes Pal - your AI referral companion

  • You can tell us about your needs and our AI engine will trawl through our collated listings to search for schemes that could be relevant to you.

  • More details on how to use the AI search tool on the Schemes Pal page.

Go to Schemes Pal
The complete Schemes Bank

We've consolidated public listings of government, VWO, self-help group schemes and made them filterable. We constantly update the list. Best viewed on Desktop. If you're on mobile tap on the Bank's main area to activate the inner scrollbar.

[Beta] Schemes Case - Engage one of our volunteers

Can't find anything you need using Schemes Pal? Trawled through Schemes Bank and no schemes exist for your needs?

  • There might be a case for further research to assist you and also to update our Schemes Bank

  • Drop us a note and our volunteers will help to search. If it is a new trend or area, we might also do more research to shed light on it.

Schemes SG is a product made by someone who cares about the social sector and wants to help those in need access assistance more quickly. We hope you find it helpful!
